Boogie is a photo magazine created by Patrick Skwiot to document skateboarding in The West Bronx and Northern Manhattan. The first issue was released in 2021 and will be released on a "when it's done" time schedule. For any questions or to request a copy of the zine, send an email to boogieskatemag@gmail.com.
